YABATECH: Shaping the future of Nigerian Publishing

Introduction: Exploring the Role of the Department in Training the Next Generation of Editors, Publishers, and Book Designers. In the heart of Lagos lies a beacon of creativity and innovation. Yaba College of Technology (YABATECH). Known for its rich history and academic excellence, the institution has become a crucial force in shaping Nigeria’s publishing industry. As the publishing landscape evolves, YABATECH is equipping its students with the skills and knowledge to become the next generation of editors, publishers, and book designers.

Education: Under Peter Obi’s leadership, schools were renovated, equipped with ICT tools, and teachers were regularly trained and paid promptly. He invested heavily in mission schools and ensured consistent WAEC support.

Health: Obi's administration revamped general hospitals, equipped them with essential medical supplies, and partnered with international health agencies for better service delivery.

Infrastructure: Roads across the state were constructed and rehabilitated. He ensured proper drainage systems and sustainable urban planning.

Security: With efficient funding and coordination, Obi restructured the state's security architecture, decreasing crime rates significantly.
